The Fastride Story

Fastride Shipping LLC is one of Dubai’s premier logistics and shipping companies, known for its personalized freight forwarding solutions that prioritize speed, safety, and reliability. With over nine years of industry experience, Fastride Shipping was officially founded in 2017 and quickly became a trusted partner for businesses in the UAE and beyond. Our customers rely on us for our in-depth understanding of the complexities involved in freight forwarding, coupled with a commitment to delivering tailored solutions.

A Journey Built on Trust

Our journey began with a simple yet ambitious goal: to create a freight forwarding company that offered seamless solutions across the Middle East and beyond. With our heart in the UAE, a global vision, and a relentless dedication to service, we have built a strong foundation by providing our clients with trustworthy logistics and shipping services tailored to meet their unique needs.
From air freight to road transport across the GCC, we focus on precision, compliance, and keeping your cargo on track, no matter what challenges arise.
