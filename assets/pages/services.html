Freight Solutions

At Fastride Shipping, we understand that logistics is more than just moving goods from point A to point B. It’s about providing solutions that are tailored to the specific needs of your business. That’s why we offer a full spectrum of freight services to cover all your shipping requirements.

Air Freight

We offer rapid and reliable air freight solutions for time-sensitive cargo. Whether you’re shipping urgent parts or sensitive goods, we provide custom solutions, including special carrier rates, dangerous goods handling, and complete customs clearance. From the moment your cargo takes off to its safe arrival, we ensure every detail is handled with the utmost care and efficiency, keeping your business moving at the speed of flight.

Road Freight

We offer unparalleled road freight services that connect your cargo across the UAE and the entire GCC region. From Saudi Arabia to Qatar and Oman, our extensive road network ensures swift, secure, and reliable transportation for all types of goods. Whether you need to move small or large shipments, we provide flexible solutions that meet your delivery deadlines. Our fleet is equipped to handle diverse cargo, including volumetric and project shipments, ensuring every journey is completed with precision.

Sea Freight

Navigating international waters can be complex, but Fastride’s expertise ensures seamless sea freight solutions. With our vast network of international shipping lanes and partnerships, we ensure smooth sailing from origin to destination. From Full Container Load (FCL) to Less than Container Load (LCL) shipping, we manage every step of the process with precision. We also handle customs inspections, break bulk cargo, and specialized approvals, simplifying the logistics process for you.
